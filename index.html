Our Mission

We here at Wicklow Heather Honey are passionate about bees and beekeeping. We want to encourage the next generation of beekeepers and if you have any beekeeping questions we would be happy to help, find our contact details on our contact page here.

All our honey is 100% natural, produced by native Irish bees who collect their nectar from the heather plants that grow in the foothills of the Wicklow Mountains.

Research has shown that heather honey has comparable health benefits to manuka honey and because all our honey is locally produced you'll not only reap the health benefits but you'll also be kinder to the environment.

About us

We here at Wicklow Heather Honey have been beekeeping in Wicklow for 3 generations and the passion and skills have been passed down to each subsequent generation. Our head beekeeper Adam has been beekeeping for over forty years and is a member of the Irish Beekeepers Association.

As well as beekeeping and producing natural heather honey we also provide talks and training to new beekeepers and people interested in making their gardens more bee friendly, visit here to get in touch with us.
